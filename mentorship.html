Mentorship for INTernational Students (MINT)

MINT is a peer mentorship program that pairs incoming international students with student mentors, and is open to all international undergraduates, transfers, exchange, and graduate studdents that wish to ease their transition into Cornell.

The program seeks to connect students with the broader international community, as well as available resources to faciliate personal, academic, and professional interests.
