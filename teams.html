Executive Board

Sergio Limandibhratha
President

Sergio is a senior from Indonesia majoring in Government & Information Science. He joined ISU because he was passionate and curious about the diverse international community on campus and has grown to love the strong connections created in ISU. Sergio really appreciates how much ISU can reach all sorts of people and make a positive impact on the Cornell community.

Huy Le
Vice President of the Finance Committee

Huy is a junior Information Science major from Saigon, Vietnam. In addition to ISU, Huy is involved in Hack4Impact and the Cornell Design & Tech Initiative. Huy joined ISU during his freshman fall and has been involved in the finance and publicity teams ever since. He loves ISU's welcoming and friendly community.

Stephen Yang
Vice President of the Advocacy Committee

Stephen is a senior from Taiwan majoring in Communication and Information Science. He’s a UX/HCI researcher studying participatory AI and mobile social media. He runs a column in the Cornell Daily Sun where he writes about the shifting landscape of technology and culture. Stephen joined ISU with a focus on fostering the inclusion of international students from underrepresented backgrounds. On campus, you can usually find him at Temple of Zeus.

Megan Cabaero
Vice President of the Planning Committee

Megan is a junior studying Human Development. Being born in the Philippines and having grown up in Singapore, she initially joined ISU to find community at Cornell. Joining ISU has been one of the best experiences in her college life as she has been able to meet, befriend, and learn from incredible people from all over the world! When she isn’t working on ISU, she also illustrates for GUAC Travel magazine, researches for her the Health Design Innovations Lab, and plays spider solitaire.

Ian Huang
Vice President of the Publicity Committee

Ian is a sophomore from Taiwan majoring in Hotel Administration. In addition to ISU, he holds leadership positions in Design Consulting at Cornell and the Cornell Club of Taiwan and is involved in Crème de Cornell and Hotel Ezra Cornell. ISU eased Ian's transition to the States, meeting many close friends and people from a similar background. The ISU community is tight-knit and enjoyable in both social and professional settings.

Yvie Cai
Vice President of the Internal Operations Committee

Yvie is a sophomore from Canada majoring in Biology & Society. In addition to ISU, she is involved with Phi Gamma Nu Business Fraternity. Yvie joined ISU to find an international community. The ISU family has become Yvie's "home away from home".

Khuslen Khosbayar
Director of Executive Board

Khuslen Khosbayar is a senior from Mongolia studying InfoSic and Comm. She joined ISU her first semester at Cornell and made so many great friends and memories through the org since then.She loves the supportive and family-like environment of ISU and hopes to create the same for new members.
