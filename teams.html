Zoya Mohsin
President

Zoya is a senior majoring in Environmental Science & Development Sociology. She is from Malawi and Kenya, and currently lives in Mombasa, Kenya! Zoya is also involved with the Womxn of Colour Coalition. She joined ISU to meet more international people. ISU allows people to meet people whom they would not have met in other circumstances and it's a lovely place to learn a lot about yourself and others.

He joined ISU because he believes that it's important to embrace diversity and internationalism as the world is now more connected than ever before. Bee loves ISU because it allowed him to meet people from all over the world.
